About Our Whacky Game

Shopping Spree: Extreme!!! is made for casual gamers looking to have a good laugh (solo or with up to three friends!). Make your way around the shopping center to collect items on your randomly-generated shopping list. But be extremely careful; one slip up and you'll be paying for a broken item! Avoid absurdly grumpy shoppers or simply shove them out of the way! Various other obstacles get in your way as you carefully fill your cart and make it to checkout without amassing a huge amount of debt!

Planning to be released in 2023, this game will be available on Steam for PC(Windows) only, but will also have full controller support!
